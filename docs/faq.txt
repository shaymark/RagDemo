AquaBot Frequently Asked Questions
====================================

Q: Does AquaBot work with saltwater (reef) aquariums?
A: Yes! The Sensor Hub supports both freshwater and saltwater/reef aquariums. The salinity
measurement is available in saltwater mode. Reef-specific parameters like calcium and alkalinity
will be supported via the AutoDose module launching Q3 2026.

Q: My sensor is showing an ammonia spike. What should I do?
A: Do a 25-30% water change immediately using dechlorinated water at the same temperature.
Reduce feeding for the next 48 hours. Check if any fish have died and remove them quickly.
Add beneficial bacteria (e.g., Seachem Stability). If the spike persists after 24 hours,
contact our support team through the app.

Q: How do I connect the Sensor Hub to Wi-Fi?
A: Open the AquaBot app, tap "Add Device", hold the Sensor Hub button for 3 seconds until
it blinks blue, then follow the in-app instructions. It connects to 2.4GHz Wi-Fi networks.
5GHz networks are not supported. Make sure your router allows new device connections.

Q: Can I use AquaBot without the Pro subscription?
A: Yes. The free tier includes real-time monitoring, 30-day data history, push notifications
for critical alerts, and the smart feeder control. Pro adds predictive AI alerts, unlimited
history, and multi-tank management. Most hobbyists are happy with the free tier.

Q: How accurate are the sensors?
A: pH accuracy is ±0.1 units. Temperature accuracy is ±0.2°C. Ammonia accuracy is ±0.05 ppm.
We recommend calibrating the pH sensor every 3 months using the included calibration solution.
Replacement sensor probes are $19 each and last approximately 12-18 months.

Q: What happens if my internet goes down?
A: The Sensor Hub stores up to 7 days of readings locally. Once connectivity is restored,
it syncs all stored data. The app shows cached data offline but real-time alerts require
an internet connection.

Q: Can I share my aquarium data with my vet or aquarium store?
A: Yes. Go to Settings > Data Sharing and generate a shareable link valid for 7 or 30 days.
The link shows a read-only dashboard with all your recent water parameters. No login required
for the recipient.

Q: Is my data private?
A: Your water parameter data is stored encrypted on AWS servers in the US. We do not sell
personal data to third parties. Anonymized aggregate data may be used to improve our AI models.
You can export or delete all your data at any time from Settings > Account > Data Management.
