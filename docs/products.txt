AquaBot Products
================

1. AquaBot Sensor Hub (Hardware)
---------------------------------
The Sensor Hub is a waterproof device that attaches to the inside of any aquarium.
It measures: temperature, pH, ammonia (NH3), nitrite (NO2), nitrate (NO3), dissolved oxygen,
and water hardness (GH/KH). Readings are sent every 60 seconds to the AquaBot cloud.
Compatible with freshwater and saltwater aquariums from 5 to 500 gallons.
Battery life: 6 months. Charges via USB-C.
Price: $89 one-time purchase.

2. AquaBot Smart Feeder (Hardware)
------------------------------------
An automatic feeder that connects to the AquaBot app.
Supports pellets, flakes, and freeze-dried foods. Holds up to 200ml of food.
Programmable feeding schedules: up to 8 feedings per day.
Portion control: fine, medium, large. Vacation mode dispenses minimal food when you're away.
Price: $49 one-time purchase.

3. AquaBot App (Software - Free)
----------------------------------
Available on iOS and Android. The app is free forever with any hardware purchase.
Features: real-time dashboard, historical charts (30 days), push alerts, feeding schedules,
water change reminders, fish health diary, community forum access.
Works offline with cached data.

4. AquaBot Pro Subscription (Software)
----------------------------------------
$9.99/month or $79/year.
Adds: AI anomaly detection with early warnings (up to 48 hours before a crash),
unlimited data history, automated water change scheduling, API access for custom integrations,
premium support (response within 2 hours), and up to 5 aquarium profiles.
Cancel anytime.

5. AquaBot AutoDose (Hardware - Coming Soon Q3 2026)
------------------------------------------------------
An automatic dosing pump that adds fertilizer, calcium, or other supplements on a schedule.
Controlled directly from the AquaBot app. Supports up to 4 liquid reservoirs.
Pre-order price: $129.
