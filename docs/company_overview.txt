AquaBot Company Overview
========================

AquaBot was founded in 2021 by marine biologist Dr. Sofia Chen and software engineer Marcus Rivera.
The company is headquartered in San Diego, California, with a remote team of 45 employees.

AquaBot's mission is to make aquarium keeping effortless through intelligent automation and AI-powered
water quality monitoring. We believe every fish deserves a healthy home.

Our platform connects to smart sensors placed in your aquarium and uses machine learning to detect
problems before they harm your fish. The system can automatically adjust temperature, lighting,
feeding schedules, and alert owners to dangerous pH or ammonia spikes.

AquaBot has served over 50,000 aquarium hobbyists and 300 professional aquariums since launch.
We won the 2023 PetTech Innovation Award and were featured in Wired magazine.

Core values:
- Fish First: every decision prioritizes animal welfare
- Simplicity: powerful tech that anyone can use
- Transparency: open data, no hidden fees
- Community: we learn from our users
